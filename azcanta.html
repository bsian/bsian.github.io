Verdict: Buy

This card sees more play in eternal formats than standard, but is a house in the decks that can play it. On its own, the enchantment is powerful library manipulation and card selection, but combined with the myriad of cantrips and self-milling cards in eternal formats, it is not uncommon for this card to flip to its land side on turn 4 or earlier. The flipside is ridiculous, giving you even stronger card selection every turn and also providing a turn of ramp, a relatively unique ability in blue. This card will be good in constructed for a long time and can be slotted into most blue EDH decks.
