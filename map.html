Verdict: Sell

This card has the fairly unique effect of initially being card filtering then transitioning into card advantage later in the game. Flexible cards like this tend to see play somewhere, and the card draw capabilities make this card playable out of the sideboard in aggressive decks. However, the effect is slow and narrow enough to make this the only place this will see play. The Treasures mechanic is limited to Ixalan block, meaning there will likely be no further support for it outside of these sets, making potential casual synergy unlikely. I do not see this card seeing much play, especially once better options become available through this standard cycle.
Comparable Cards
     

 

 Key is another fringe playable card advantage engine in aggressive decks, but is faster and has more synergy with the gameplan of aggressive decks. The card has never been above $1.00.
