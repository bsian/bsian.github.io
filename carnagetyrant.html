Verdict: Sell

This card was one of the most hyped of the pre-release period with a pre-order price of around $25.00, but has failed to live up to the hype and produce as many standard tournament results as expected. This will see play in standard, but not as much as previously thought, and is unplayable in eternal formats. It does not slot into many popular EDH decks, so I don't see this card retaining much value after its standard rotation.
