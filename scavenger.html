Verdict: Keep

This won't be a format defining 3 drop, but can be slotted into most green decks, and is especially powerful in meta games where the graveyard plays a role. Cards like this that can be played both main and side board in a wide variety of decks tend to maintain a medium price point throughout their time in standard. This doesn't seem to stack up to other options in eternal formats, but could be a fringe playable, especially in Collected Company decks. It should stay around its current price, if not increase until its rotation.
