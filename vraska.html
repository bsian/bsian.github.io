Verdict: Keep

This card may be expensive both in terms of mana and dollars, but shows no signs of being played less and is so far the most impressive card of Ixalan in standard. Not seeing eternal play, but as with most planeswalkers should be playable in all EDH decks in its color and will be popular among casual players even after its rotation. This card is unlikely to decline in value in the near future and shows potential for a value increase before it rotates out.
